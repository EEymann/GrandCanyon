Journey through the Grand Canyon National Park

Grand Canyon Village, Arizona

The Grand Canyon National Park is one of the most beautiful natural attractions of the world. The canyon spans a breathtaking 277 miles (446 km) long, is up to 18 miles (29 km) wide, and has a depth of over one mile (6,093 feet or 1,857 meters).[5] The entirety of the canyon sits within the Grand Canyon National Park, Navajo Nation, the Havasupai Indian Reservation, the Hualapai Indian Reservation, the Grand Canyon-Parashant National Monument, and the Kaibab National Forest
